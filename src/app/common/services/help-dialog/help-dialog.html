How do I manage my notifications?

If you installed the android app:

  • Long-press the app on the home-screen
  • Go to Site settings
  • Disallow/Allow notifications

If you are on a web-browser:

  • Press CTRL+T
  • Type "How to disable/enable notifications on [browser]"
  • Press Enter

Something isn't working!

You can always clear site data by going to the site settings. If you have installed the android app, you can uninstall the app like any other, and reinstall it by revisiting the website.

If the issue persists, please ask Till Riedel or Erik Pescara. They'd probably love to help you with your problem.
